About UofTMHA

The University of Toronto Mental Health Student Association (UofTMHA) was established in 2016 and works to link students with mental health resources. I created and a deployed a frontend and backend website for University of Toronto Mental Health Student Association (UofTMHA), using Next.js deployed in Vercel, Sanity (CMS) for the blog, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS, and used Prisma framework for the database. Performed SEO optimization, implemented Google API, and migrated the React app into Next.js.
